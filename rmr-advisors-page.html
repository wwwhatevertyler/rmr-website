Why advisors join

Why advisors join Roll My Retirement

We act as a focused growth partner for advisors who want qualified rollover opportunities without building a full outbound sales engine.

People in transition

We focus on individuals navigating job changes, inherited assets, dissatisfaction with a current advisor, and rollover decisions.

Qualified before connection

We pre-screen opportunities before introducing them to your team, helping reduce wasted time and improve fit.

Built for focused growth

We support the front end of business development so your team can stay focused on converting and serving clients.

Selective network

This is a curated advisor network, not an open marketplace. We prioritize quality, fit, and professionalism.

Standards

Who we look for

We review applications holistically based on practice quality, client experience, responsiveness, rollover expertise, regulatory standing, and overall fit for our network.

We are building a high-quality advisor network, not a volume-based directory.

  • Retirement and rollover planning focus
  • Active registration or equivalent licensing
  • Clean compliance history
  • Strong client service model
  • Ability to respond quickly to opportunities
